Ordering Questions

8 Questions

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our menu, select your desired items, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your delivery address and payment information to complete your order.

Pro Tip: Create an account to save your favorite orders for faster checkout next time.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

You can modify or cancel your order within 10 minutes of placing it by contacting our customer support team. After this window, your order enters preparation and cannot be changed.

Note: For immediate assistance, call our support line at +91 9876543210.

What are your operating hours?

Our kitchen operates from 8:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily. However, delivery hours may vary based on your location and local regulations.

Day Opening Time Closing Time
Monday - Friday 8:00 AM 11:00 PM
Saturday - Sunday 9:00 AM 11:00 PM
